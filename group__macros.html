◆ HIF_RESERVE_FAC

#define HIF_RESERVE_FAC   5

reserve memory factor for factorization

Note
default is 5

We reserve HIF_RESERVE_FAC times nnz(A) for the L and U factors for factorization. We found that this is good enough for most problems without the need of a reallocation.
